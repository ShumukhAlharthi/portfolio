Project Overview

OptiScan is an AI-based medical diagnosis system developed to assist ophthalmologists in detecting retinal diseases using Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) scans. The system classifies retinal conditions into: CNV, DME, Drusen, and Normal cases.
